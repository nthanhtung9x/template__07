ThaTu Hotel

Every one of us at the luxury ThaTu Saigon Hotel in Vietnam wants to make your stay with us a pleasure, whether you’re here on business or leisure … or even to get married. Great food is an important part of any stay and we have three excellent restaurants serving Chinese, Japanese and international cuisines.
We make work a little easier with complimentary Internet service in your room and throughout the hotel, flexible venues for meetings and conferences of all sizes, and a business center that can handle all your needs, from office supplies and photocopying to secretarial and translation support.
We make holidays more enjoyable with a fitness center equipped with full range of professional Technogym equipment, a hydrotherapy area with massage showers, steam bath, sauna and Jacuzzi, an outdoor swimming pool, a tennis court, and spa and massage services. Sightseeing and shopping are made easier with our private limousine service and a complimentary city shuttle service that makes it easy to move around Ho Chi Minh City.
And we love weddings. If you’re getting married, we can make everything you dream of come true.
